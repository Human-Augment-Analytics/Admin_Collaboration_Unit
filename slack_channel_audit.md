## Observed Patterns

- **Image Processing** and **Methods** are the two channels with the most consistent non-scheduling activity. Both are advised by Steve Mussmann. Both have had active unit managers posting structured agendas.
- **R-Scripting** has the least substantive activity of any channel. After the ecological modeling group left in Sep 2025, there was one PPT shared (Sep 22), two logistics questions (Oct 3, Oct 20), and then no activity until the Jan 2026 When2Meet. No faculty advisor posts after Aug 2025.
- **3-D Vision** had concentrated technical discussion in May–Jun 2025 (paper sharing, framework testing coordination, Zotero setup). Activity dropped sharply after summer. No substantive posts from Oct 2025 through the audit date — only joins and scheduling.
- Across all four channels, the majority of non-join/leave posts are meeting logistics (scheduling, When2Meet, calendar invites, cancellations). Presentation file shares are the second most common post type.
- Multiple members in 3-D Vision and R-Scripting posted work conflicts preventing meeting attendance (Steve Foryoung, John Hagood, Nikita Angarski, Vanessa Zhou, Tadeas Lobreis).
- Both Image Processing and R-Scripting underwent unit restructuring in Sep 2025 (spatial camera group moved out of Image Processing and into R-Scripting; ecological modeling left R-Scripting).
- In spring 2026, new members in Image Processing needed manual re-invites to meetings (Heba Elwazzan Feb 27, Rebecca Rivera Mar 5).
- Emily Weigel (R-Scripting advisor) posted only during the Aug 2025 scheduling window. Supratik Mukhopadhyay (3-D Vision advisor) posted only during May 2025. Steve Mussmann posted regularly across both semesters in Image Processing and Methods.
- **Active-to-current member ratios (admins excluded):** Image Processing 9/64 (14%), R-Scripting 3/24 (13%), 3-D Vision 7/39 (18%), Methods 7/47 (15%). In all four channels, over 80% of current non-admin members have never posted more than once.

## Methodology Notes

- Member counts are based on join/leave messages visible in the full Slack channel history as of March 25, 2026.
- "Active members" are defined as users who posted more than 1 non-join/leave message in the main channel AND have not left the channel AND are not in the admin exclusion list.
- Admin exclusion list: Charles R Clark, John Hagood, Bree Shi, Ali Sean Khan, Cody Henderson, Michael Bock, Anisha Rai, Ichha Manandhar. These users appear across multiple unit channels in organizational/administrative roles rather than as unit researchers.
- Thread-only replies are not counted in main channel post totals, so users who only replied in threads may be undercounted.
- Raw data for all 458 logged events is available in the companion file: `channel_activity.csv`
